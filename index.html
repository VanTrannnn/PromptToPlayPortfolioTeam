Humada's Stories

Community Storytelling page for Huynh Man Dat High School for the Gifted

What is Humada's Stories?

"Humada's Stories" is a community platform, operating as a confession and storytelling hub. The page acts as a bridge, connecting current students and alumni, giving them a voice and a space to share their memorable high school experiences.

Project Overview

  • Project Type: Community Storytelling
  • Target Audience: High School Students, Alumni
  • Key Metrics: High engagement, regular submissions
  • My Role: UI/UX, Content Creator, Design Asset Management

Special Series
